Built for the People, by the People

Three Uilas was started by local people who understand what our communities need. We wanted affordable utility vehicles for zipping around subdivisions, farm lots, and unimproved roads, and were shocked at our options. That is why we decided to offer an affordable alternative specifically customized for Hawaiʻi.

Our electric cargo trikes feature steel construction, reliable electric systems, and practical features that make everyday work on the land easier and more efficient. The components are modular and generic, and easily replaceable.

When you choose Three Uilas, you're getting a quality vehicle backed by our commitment to local communities with reliable service and a solid 2-year parts warranty.
